From his clinic in Newcastle, Duncan provides effective chiropractic treatment and management for conditions including:

  • Lower back pain and Sciatica
  • Neck pain
  • Whiplash
  • Migraine and headaches
  • Shoulder and arm pain
  • Hip and knee complaints

Specially trained in pregnancy related conditions including Symphysis Pubis Dysfunction (SPD) and pelvic alignment.

Duncan graduated from The Welsh Institute Of Chiropractic at the university of Glamorgan with a BSc (Hons) in Chiropractic in 2003. He returned back up to his native North East where he has worked in several private practices across the region.

Duncan provides effective chiropractic treatment for a range of conditions and is specially trained in pregnancy related conditions including Symphysis Pubis Dysfunction (SPD) and pelvic alignment.

He is registered with the General Chiropractic Council and is a member of the British Chiropractic Association. He currently practices from his clinic in Newcastle.
