We always welcome new patients to the clinic

On your first visit we will carry out a new patient consultation. This includes recording your medical history and a thorough physical examination. A treatment plan will be formulated and explained, and treatment can begin on this visit. This will take approximately 60 minutes.

  • Please wear loose comfortable clothing and bring a list of any medications you may be taking.
  • If you have had any X-ray,MRI or CT examinations recently and have access to the reports, please bring a copy.
  • If you have time please fill in the new patient questionaire If not, allow yourself an extra 5 minutes before your appointment to fill it in.
